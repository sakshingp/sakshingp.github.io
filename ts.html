Our Story
Ours is a story of 3 students who did not like the way we were being educated, so we decided to change the definition of education. The sapling of TET was sown to break the stereotypes associated with education by broadening its horizons. Ideally, education should transcend the boundaries of academics to truly and holistically develop an individual. It should also nurture awareness and social responsiveness while cogently countering the forces of the diverse social-cultural backgrounds, existing in our society. The community aims to achieve this wholesome, inclusive vision.
The Impact That we have made:
Here are few of the campaigns we have created to raise awareness around social issues

Walk in her Shoes

An initiative where men walk in women’ shoes both metaphorically and literally.

Jo Mera hai vo mera hai

An attempt to spread awareness on Women Safety through a series of freezemobs across 20+ cities.

Being A Woman

An annual event which celebrates the spirit of women in its purest forms.

Nadaan Parindey

A series of freezemobs and flashmobs to generate awareness on Child Labour in India.

Aaghaz

An initiative to revive street theatre in the hearts of the masses and disseminate information about various social issues through a series of street plays.

Project Emerge

A campaign that aims to provide socio-economically disadvantaged students with necessary training and material. Collaborated with over 10 NGOs.

Marks Do Not Matter

A campaign highlighting how success is independent of the marks you score.

Unmute

A digital campaign to start conversations around the norms set by the society and the challenges faced by the youth.
